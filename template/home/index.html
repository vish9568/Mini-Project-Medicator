About Us

It might have happened so many times that you or someone yours need doctor's help immediately, but they are not available due to some reasons. The Disease predition system is an end user support and online consultation project. Here, we propose a web application that slow users to get instant guidance on their disease through an inteligent system. The application is fed with various details and the disease associated with those details. The application allows users to share their disease realated issues.It then processes user specific details to check for various illness that could be associated with it. Here we use some intelligent Machine Learning techniques to guess the most accurate illness that could be associated with patient's details. Based on result patient can contact doctor accordingly for further treatment.
